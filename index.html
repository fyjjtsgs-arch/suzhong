Bio

I am a tenure-track associate professor in Mathematics at Shanghai Jiao Tong University.
I completed my Ph.D. in Mathematics at UCSD in June 2022, under the supervision of Jiawang Nie. From August 2022 to July 2025, I was a VAP in Texas A&M University, under the mentorship of Jianxin Zhou.
My research interests primarily lie in polynomial optimization. Specifically, I develop and apply these techniques to tackle complex problems in stochastic programming, equilibrium models, and data science.
I am currently recruiting motivated graduate students and postdocs. If you are interested in my research, please feel free to contact me via my SJTU email (I currently have no access to my TAMU email).
